## Naming Convention

All photos follow the pattern: `category-description-number.jpg`

**Categories:**
- `branding` - Logos, graphics
- `exterior` - Building/storefront shots
- `food` - All food photography
- `interior` - Inside restaurant shots
- `team` - People/family photos
- `truck` - Food truck related

**Sub-categories for food:**
- `chicken`, `beef`, `falafel` - Protein type
- `wrap`, `plate` - Dish type
- `prep` - Preparation/action shots
- `spread` - Multiple dishes
